🎉 Welcome to the Strategy Wizard! 🎉

Here you can design your strategy and set your own rules for how to play.

📝 You will be prompted with forms where you can assign values to your pieces and decide how to evaluate chess positions.

💾 After finishing the setup, you can save this strategy as a "card". This card can be used later to challenge other players or face off against AI bots.

🧠 Be wise with your decisions... every choice counts in the game!
